Using the Shutter Foldable TM by Dinah Zike Phonemic Awareness/Phonics/Spelling Application Use the Shutter Foldable to review contractions and compound words with students. The outside panels can be used to demonstrate the two parts of the word. Students can use the middle inside panel to write out the contraction or complete compound word. Comprehension Application There are many ways to use the Shutter Foldable to review and study comprehension skills. Larger paper can be used so that a small group or a class can create one of these for literacy study. Consider having students retell or summarize the story on the middle inside panel. Then have them use the outer panels to analyze the following: • Facts and Opinions (for nonﬁction) • Cause and Effect • Before and After • Fantasy and Reality • Pros and Cons (for persuasion) • Problem and Solution • Compare and Contrast • Phonics for Primary Grades Large Shutter Foldables can be stored in an empty (and clean!) pizza box. 14 Foldables
