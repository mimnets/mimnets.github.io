Layered Book Foldable TM Directions by Dinah Zike Materials: • two sheets of 8½ 11 paper • glue Directions: 1. Stack two sheets of paper so that the back sheet is one inch higher than the front sheet. 2. Bring the bottom of both sheets upward and align the edges so that all of the layers or tabs are the same distance apart. 3. When all tabs are an equal distance apart, fold the papers and crease well. 4. Open the papers and glue them together along the valley, or inner center fold, or staple them along the mountain. If you need more layers, use additional sheets of paper. Make the tabs smaller than one inch. Use this Foldable to Foldables 23
