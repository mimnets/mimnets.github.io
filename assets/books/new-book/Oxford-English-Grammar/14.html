Adjectives working with nouns The man in the hat could be either of these: The short man in the floppy hat could only be one of them. It’s the adjectives short and floppy that make the difference: Adjectives work with nouns, describing people and things. Which noun does short work with? Which noun does floppy work with? Adjectives can be used in a suspicious parcel a wet, chilly afternoon front of nouns to make noun phrases. secret documents a bright, warm, spring morning Adjectives give many different sorts of information about nouns.
