Pronouns words for nouns Pronouns are words for people, animals, and things - just like nouns. he/him These pronouns are called personal pronouns. Some pronouns are for female things only she her Some are for male things only he him we/us I'm telling you he's a spy and he's following us Pronouns by themselves are blank. They can mean almost anything. So when you use pronoun you must make it clear what, or who, it does mean.
