Exclamations have feeling! An exclamation is a sentence said with feeling, like surprise or amusement. What a funny looking man! How stupid he looks! Exclamations often begin with How ... or What . . . and in writing they always end with an exclamation mark. Questions ask things Many questions begin with: who what which when where why You can call these Wh- questions. Who is that man? What's he doing? Why is he hiding? There is another kind of question that asks if something is so or not. Is he still there? Can you see him? Do you think he's a spy? Questions like these are called Yes/No questions. .
